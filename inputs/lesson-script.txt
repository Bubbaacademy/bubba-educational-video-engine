Photosynthesis 101

[scene]
Welcome to Bubba Academy. Today we're going to explore how plants turn sunlight into food.

[scene]
Photosynthesis is the process plants use to convert light energy into chemical energy.

[scene title="Plants need four things" items="Sunlight,Water,Carbon Dioxide,Chlorophyll"]
For photosynthesis to happen, a plant needs all four of these ingredients working together.

[scene title="The Process" nodes="CO2 + H2O,Chloroplast,Glucose + O2"]
Plants take in carbon dioxide and water, transform them inside the chloroplast using sunlight, and produce glucose and oxygen.

[scene]
The most important thing to remember is that sunlight is the energy source — without it, the entire process stops.

[scene]
[warning] Do not assume plants only photosynthesize. At night they also respire — taking in oxygen and releasing carbon dioxide, just like animals.
